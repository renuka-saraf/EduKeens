History of Harvard School of Medicine

Established in 1782, Harvard Medical School began with a handful of students and a faculty of three. The first classes were held in Harvard Hall in Cambridge, long before the School’s iconic quadrangle was built in Boston. With each passing decade, the School’s faculty and trainees amassed knowledge and influence, shaping medicine in the United States and beyond.

Campus of DU

Harvard Medical School’s Longwood campus is in the heart of one of the nation’s most vibrant cities. Boston’s unique charm and character emerges from its central role in American history and the easy access it offers to a cosmopolitan abundance of personal, social, and professional opportunities.

Admission to Harvard Medical School's MD program is highly selective. There are 165 total spots for each incoming class, with 135 spots in the Pathways curriculum and 30 spots in the HST program.While both use a single application, each curricular track independently evaluates applicants. For the MD Class of 2023, 6,815 candidates applied and 227 were admitted (3.3%).

Doctor of Medicine(M.D)

    University Admission Requirements

    Official Transcripts

  • MCAT (Medical College Admission Test) scores
  • Subjects:
  • Behavioral Sciences-
    Encouraged to complete coursework in Behavioral Sciences (e.g. psychology, sociology, etc).
  • Biology-
    1.One year with lab experience (one semester of lab experience should be with corresponding coursework; however, second semester lab may be fulfilled by independent laboratory based research) is required.
    2.Should include cellular and molecular aspects
    3.AP credits cannot be used; however, if students have earned college credit for biology through AP coursework, upper level courses in biology will satisfy this requirement.
  • Chemistry/Biochemistry-
    1.Two years of chemistry (four courses) including inorganic chemistry, organic chemistry, and biochemistry are required.
    2.Lab experience required
    3.AP credits that enable you to take a higher-level course will satisfy one semester of inorganic chemistry
  • Physics-One year is required.
  • Math-Encouraged to complete one year of coursework in mathematics, including one semester each of calculus and statistics (preferably biostatistics)
  • Writing-One year is required.
  • In addition to these requirements for all MD candidates, HST candidates are required to demonstrate competency in upper-level mathematics, biochemistry, molecular biology, and calculus-based physics.
